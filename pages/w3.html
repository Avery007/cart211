History of Net Art

The Internet art first emerged in the early 1990s. As the technology evolves, network enables people to deliver information in varies way, and the internet becomes not just a platform to share resurces, but the way how people organize their materials becomes a kind of art---today we have videos, images, medias, and all of them are integrated into the internet. The network seems to become "alive" as if it has own culture--a culture somewhat reprsents our society.

However,I think it is important to point out that the internet art doesnt really reflect our real culture because the network is easier to be manipulated.

Technologies make fake news flood into the network when it is so easy to modify images and videos.

The network art is also influenced largely by commercial avtivities.While our life is surrounded by advertisements, they tend to be exaggerated and unreal.

So perharps one noticeable feature of the net art is that people are stuck into a kind of virtual reality. We have get used to view the world through internet while often forgetting that what we find on internet may not be real.
