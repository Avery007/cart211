A world of Net Art





The History


According to the reading, When the internet first showed up, people didnt expect much from its function other than a communication tool. Yet the term "net art" accidentially came to the mind of Slovenian artist Vuk Cosic in the 1995. Then the idea of online art and communication started to go viral. During the 1990s, Internet helped the artists extend their potential as they could share challenging ideas without being marginalized by the community.
Note:rhizome is one of the earliest websites dedicated to new media art.



Cyberfeminism


In 1997, the net.art exploded and several playful works were created. At the same time net.art allowed some female artists to display their advanced skills. When the Net.art platform mingled with the feminism during 1990s, it gave birth to the term "Cyberfeminist", who considered the Internet as a tool of feminist liberation.

VNS Matrix is one of the first websites exhibiting the conception of cyberfeminism.
Based on this reference: The History of cyberfeminism Personally, I feel the idea of Cyberfeminism is more about transhumanism rather than feminism, or in other words, it is like feminism is leaded to the direction of roboticization ranther than focusing on feminine power itself.
The name VNS Matrix reminds me of the movie The Matrix. And the label New Brave Girls on their poster could be linked to the New Brave World.
